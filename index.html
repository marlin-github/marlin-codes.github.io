About Me

 I am third-year PhD student from Department of Computer Science and Engineering;
The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) advised by Prof. Irwin King. I am interested
in the (temporal) graph representation learning, hyperbolic/sphere/semi-Riemannian space
embedding, and machine learning theory. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Graph theory for graph embedding
  • Temporal network embedding
  • Hyperbolic represenation learning
  • Recommender system
  • Knowledge graph
  • Graph-based Drug discovery

Welcome outstanding undergraduates and Master/PhD students to visit our WISC laboratory
and cooperate with me for graph-related research. If you are interested, just drop an email
to me and Prof. King.
