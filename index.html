About Me

 I am third-year PhD (2019-) student of the Computer Science and Engineering department in the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) advised by Irwin King.

Research Interest

 I am interested in the (temporal) graph representation learning, hyperbolic/sphere/semi-Riemannian space embedding,
machine learning theory. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Graph spectral theory for graph represenation learning
  • Temporal graph learning
  • Non-Euclidean space for graph represenation learning
  • Graph-based recommender system
  • Knowledge graph
  • Graph-based Drug discovery
